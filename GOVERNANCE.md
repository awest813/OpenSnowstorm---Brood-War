# Governance

OpenSnowstorm is an open-source project maintained by its contributors.

## Decision making

- **Default**: decisions are made by rough consensus in issues/PRs.
- **Maintainer call**: when consensus isn’t reachable, maintainers decide with the project’s long-term health in mind (determinism/correctness > aesthetics).

## Maintainers

- The maintainers are the GitHub users with write access to the repository.

## Project priorities

When trade-offs arise, we generally prioritize:

1. **Determinism and correctness**
2. **Compatibility with Brood War behavior and tooling**
3. **Debuggability and testability**
4. **Maintainability and modularity**
5. **Performance**, once correctness is protected by tests
